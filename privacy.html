Privacy Policy for Pat A Pug

Last updated: 7 May 2026

Pat A Pug is a simple game for iPhone and iPad.

Pat A Pug does not collect, store, use, share, or sell personal information.

The app does not require users to create an account. The app does not collect names, email addresses, location information, contacts, photos, health information, payment information, or other personal data.

The app does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

The app does not transmit gameplay information to us or to any third party.

If you contact us for support by email, we will use your email address only to respond to your enquiry.

If the app’s privacy practices change in a future version, this policy will be updated before that version is released.

For support or privacy questions, contact: PatAPug.app@gmail.com
